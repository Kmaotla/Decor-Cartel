My Journey

Founded in 2024, I started this business out of a deep love for bringing people together through beautifully curated picnics and unforgettable experiences.

As a one-woman business, I personally handle every detail—from planning to execution—ensuring a personalized touch in every event. My goal is to take the stress off your shoulders so you can simply enjoy the moment with your loved ones.

Our Values

At Decor Cartel, we believe in the power of beauty to bring people together. We are dedicated to creating experiences that not only look amazing but also feel magical.

  • Client Welcome: Final checks are done to make sure everything is perfect.
  • Creativity: From elegant floral arrangements to cozy boho aesthetics.
  • Community: We foster connection and meaningful moments.
  • Sustainability: We minimize waste and use eco-friendly materials.
